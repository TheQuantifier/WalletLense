Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the Security section on your Profile page.

Where can I view my financial records?

Your records are under the Records tab in the main navigation.

How do I export my data?

Go to Records and use the Export option to download a CSV for budgeting, accounting, or taxes.

Why don’t my charts match my records?

Check your selected date range and filters—charts only reflect the currently filtered records.

How do I delete or edit a transaction?

Open Records, select the transaction, and choose Edit or Delete from the actions menu.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@WiseWallet.app
